• "John 3:16"
• "Romans 8:28" 
• "First Corinthians 13:4"
• "John chapter 3 verse 16"
• "Psalm 23 verse 1"
• "Genesis 1:1"

CASTING BEHAVIOR:
📱 Phone: Shows controls + verse
📺 Cast Screen: Shows only verse (large, readable)
🎭 Dual screen mode for presentations
